description=[*]Optionally integrates very well with [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3532685233]PhunSprinters 2[/url] which will give you different drop rates for sprinters vs normal zeds
description=[*]Optionally integrates very well with [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3388867450]PhunZones 2[/url] which will give you zone specific settings for drop rates (eg higher drop rates in Louisville)

description=Global Settings
description=[table]
description=[tr][th]Setting[/th][th]Description[/th][/tr]
description=[tr][td][b]Default Drop Rate[/b][/td][td]The chance out of 100 of a cure carrying zed appearing. eg 10 = 10%, 5.5 = 5.5%[/td][/tr]
description=[tr][td][b]Default Sprinter Drop Rate[/b][/td][td]The chance out of 100 that the sprinter is packing a cure. eg 10 = 10%, 5.5 = 5.5%[/td][/tr]
description=[tr][td][b]Expired Cure Chance[/b][/td][td]The chance (out of 100) that the cure will allready be rotten which can further increase rarity. Note that if you disabled rotting below (eg Days to Rot = 0) then this value will have no affect. 0 means they will always be fresh, 50 means that half of the cures found will be expired and unusable[/td][/tr]
description=[tr][td][b]Days to Rot[/b][/td][td]Number of days the cure is good for without keeping in fridge or freezer. Set to 0 or a crazy high number to disable (default is 4 days). Note this value only applies after a restart and only to newly created items[/td][/tr]
description=[tr][td][b]Days Fresh[/b][/td][td]Number of days that the cure is considered fresh. Currently has no benefit. Default is 1 day. Note that changing this value only applies after a restart and to newly created items[/td][/tr]
description=[tr][td][b]Cure Virus[/b][/td][td]Injecting a valid cure will remove the knox virus[/td][/tr]
description=[tr][td][b]Heal Wound[/b][/td][td]Injecting a valid cure will heal any infected wound (eg instead of using anti biotics). This is different to the knox virus[/td][/tr]
description=[tr][td][b]Heal Bite[/b][/td][td]Injecting a valid cure will heal body part bite[/td][/tr]
description=[tr][td][b]Heal Scratch[/b][/td][td]Injecting a valid cure will heal any scratch[/td][/tr]
description=[tr][td][b]Debug[/b][/td][td]Threshold for global ambient light before sprinting is triggered. The higher this value, the lighter it can be to make sprinters run[/td][/tr]
description=[tr][td][b]Minimum Distance[/b][/td][td]The minimum distance of player to zed must be to process the chance of carrier (this is to prevent sudden popins)[/td][/tr]
description=[tr][td][b]Maximum Distance[/b][/td][td]The maximum distance of player to zed must be to process the chance of being a carrier (this is to improve performance trying to process zeds that are too far away)[/td][/tr]
description=[tr][td][b]Queue Size[/b][/td][td]The max number of zeds to update per tick[/td][/tr]
description=[/table]
