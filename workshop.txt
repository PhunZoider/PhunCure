description=[H2]Highlights[/H2]
description=[list]
description=[*]Designed for Multiplayer, fully compatible with Single Player
description=[*]Fully configurable drop rates
description=[*]Optional spoilage system, keep cures refrigerated to extend life
description=[*]Optional integration with [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3532685233]PhunSprinters 2[/url] for different sprinter vs normal drop rates
description=[*]Optional integration with [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3388867450]PhunZones 2[/url] for zone-specific drop rates (e.g. higher rates in Louisville)
description=[/list]

description=[H2]Global Settings[/H2]
description=[table]
description=[tr][th]Setting[/th][th]Description[/th][/tr]
description=[tr][td][b]Default Drop Rate[/b][/td][td]Chance out of 100 that a zed is carrying a cure. (10 = 10%, 5.5 = 5.5%)[/td][/tr]
description=[tr][td][b]Default Sprinter Drop Rate[/b][/td][td]Chance out of 100 that a sprinter is carrying a cure.[/td][/tr]
description=[tr][td][b]Expired Cure Chance[/b][/td][td]Chance (out of 100) that the cure is already expired. Further increases rarity. Has no effect if spoilage is disabled (Days to Rot = 0).[/td][/tr]
description=[tr][td][b]Days to Rot[/b][/td][td]Number of days the cure remains usable without refrigeration. Set to 0 or a very high value to disable spoilage. Applies only after restart and to newly created items.[/td][/tr]
description=[tr][td][b]Days Fresh[/b][/td][td]Number of days the cure is considered fresh (currently cosmetic only). Applies only after restart and to newly created items.[/td][/tr]
description=[tr][td][b]Cure Virus[/b][/td][td]Injecting a valid cure removes the Knox infection.[/td][/tr]
description=[tr][td][b]Heal Wound[/b][/td][td]Injecting a valid cure heals infected wounds (separate from the Knox virus).[/td][/tr]
description=[tr][td][b]Heal Bite[/b][/td][td]Injecting a valid cure heals bites.[/td][/tr]
description=[tr][td][b]Heal Scratch[/b][/td][td]Injecting a valid cure heals scratches.[/td][/tr]
description=[tr][td][b]Debug[/b][/td][td]Enables debug logging for troubleshooting and balancing.[/td][/tr]
description=[tr][td][b]Minimum Distance[/b][/td][td]Minimum distance between player and zed before carrier chance is processed (prevents pop-in behaviour).[/td][/tr]
description=[tr][td][b]Maximum Distance[/b][/td][td]Maximum distance for carrier processing (improves performance by ignoring distant zeds).[/td][/tr]
description=[tr][td][b]Queue Size[/b][/td][td]Maximum number of zeds processed per tick.[/td][/tr]
description=[/table]
